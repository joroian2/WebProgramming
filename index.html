Nutrition and your body's health

I'ts common sense that overeating contributes to becoming overweight or even obesity, but what most people don't think about are the problems that comes with those problems.

Overweight or obese people have an increased chance of having
  • Breathing Disorders
  • Cancers
    • Prostate Cancer(Men)
    • Bowel Cancer (Men)
    • Breast Cancer (Women)
    • Uterine Cancer (Women)
  • Coronary artery (heart) disease
  • Depression
  • Gallbladder or liver diseas




Make sure eat healthy and exercise!
